A quiet walk in Okutama

Apr 12, 2026 · TokyoLab

Okutama feels like the edge of Tokyo. The train keeps moving, the buildings become smaller, and the city slowly loses its noise.

I walked without trying to make a perfect story. Sometimes the best photographs come from not asking too much from a place.
